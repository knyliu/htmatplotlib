Bar Chart


A graphical representation using rectangular bars to compare different categories or groups.



Line Chart


A visual representation showing the relationship or trend between two variables over time or a continuous interval.



Pie Chart


A circular graph that illustrates the proportion or percentage of different categories within a dataset.



Multi-Bar


Compare with different data. Showing them together with bar chart.



Multi-Line


Compare with different data. Showing them together with line chart.




1. Please separate the two elements with a space.

2. It is forbidden to have space in an element.

3. All graph generation is based on Matplotlib.
